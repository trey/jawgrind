Jawgrind

Jawgrind is a podcast where 5 guys (Trey, Jason, Daniel, Cale, Phil) talk (mostly) about Star Trek.

So far, it's been about episodes of The Original Series, but we plan to move onto the movies with the original cast and then TNG, Deep Space 9, Voyager, and Enterprise. And then the rest of the movies. So. Basically everything. Eventually. Stick with us, won't you?

The show evolved out of a tech news podcast of sorts, but you can find the first bits of the current incarnation starting in Episode 4.
